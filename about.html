About

Filipe P. de Farias is Master Student of Teleinformatics Engineering at DETI/UFC in Brazil, with scholarship of the Post-Graduate Program PPGETI funded by FUNCAP. His research focuses on dynamical systems and pattern recognition. Filipe obtained his bachelor degree at 2022 in Telecommunications Engineering at UFC.

Filipe participated in Scientific Initiation Program PIBIC/UFC researching on observability and controlability of dynamical systems, statistical inference of ordinary differential equations and Process Control Technologies for Water Reuse Control4Reuse.
